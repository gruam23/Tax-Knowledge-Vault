# South Dakota v Wayfair

## 速览

Wayfair is a seed case note for US sales tax economic nexus. It needs the Supreme Court opinion and state-level guidance before it can be treated as reviewed.

## 裁判结论

Wayfair is the leading US sales tax case associated with economic nexus for remote sellers.

## 税务意义

It is useful for explaining why physical presence is no longer the only practical nexus concept in US state sales tax.

## 实务用途

Use this case when discussing cross-border e-commerce, marketplace rules, and state-level indirect tax exposure.
